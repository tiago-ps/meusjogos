Imperial Grace is a visual novel / otome game, focusing on politics and romance in an imaginary medieval world. As the new Empress of Calidya your choices will determine your future and your kingdom's, so rule wisely.





Play as a strong and ambitious woman, and hold an entire country in the palm of your hand. Thanks to the many choices offered throughout the game and the avatar customisation options, become the Empress you want to be!



Fall under the charm of the three love interests and their flamboyant personalities, and discover the kingdom of Calidya through colourful and well-developed secondary characters.





Guide your subjects by addressing their petitions, and navigate the crises unsettling your realm. It's up to you to tackle the many dilemmas that will change the history of Calidya... for better or for worse.



Enjoy the story with infinite resources, or spice up your game by wisely managing your kingdom's resources, making each choice even more difficult.
