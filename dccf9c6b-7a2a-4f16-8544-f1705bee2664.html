no sleep for sole is a surreal survival horror game about mental illness and abuse. You play as a young boy named Sole who stays awake at night to protect himself from the monsters he sees around his home.

 

Features:

  • A disturbing story about abuse and its consequences.
  • An hour and a half of content with multiple endings.
  • Tense survival gameplay that revolves around efficient use of resources, as well as trial and error.
  • Side-view sections that depict Sole's days.
  • Secrets and delusions.
