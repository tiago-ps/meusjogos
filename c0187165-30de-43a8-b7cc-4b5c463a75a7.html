GAMEPLAY

Zoria: Age of Shattering is a story-driven, party-based Classic RPG with base and character management. The player has control of its own base and a large roster of characters, adding a tactical layer to Every action taken.

Explore the beautiful lands of Zoria, from mountains to the ocean, towns and dungeons, follow the story and discover all its secrets.


Your outpost is your base. By upgrading and expanding your outpost, you get access to better facilities and the ability to influence more of the world around you while leveling your followers gives you the ability to explore deeper, further into the unknown.


STORY

Zoria. Home. Mother of all her inhabitants. She provides her children with food to grow, shelter to rest in and also, the harsh lessons of life, so her offspring may learn to fight and protect her gifts to them.
But men, men wanted more.

Zoria’s finest sons and daughters: intelligent, resourceful… violent. Men turned her ores into sword and daggers, and her roots into vile concoctions of poison.

Men wanted more. They craved more land and more food. So, they built their homes of stone and wood, driving out the wildlife of its woods and caves. And in those caves they found their instrument of defiance: zerrium.


ADVENTURE

All the places in the game are hand-crafted, with multiple paths and various options for exploration, depending on the party setup and their exploration abilities. Dungeons can always be revisited later, with a different party, to explore previously inaccessible areas and uncover their secrets.


RESOURCE MANAGEMENT

Exploring the world needs to be planned out carefully, and supplies stocked accordingly. Without supplies, the party cannot restore their vital resources, making any dungeon that much more dangerous. Bringing enough supplies for the journey or upgrading alchemy and cooking skills to make better use of all the land has to offer, that’s a choice for everyone to make.


TURN-BASED COMBAT

Zoria’s combat is a Tactical, Turn-based confrontation, designed around the attributes of each class: special abilities, resistances, deadly ranged attacks or strong AOE defenses, healing and much more. But Zoria is a living world, so choose your battlegrounds wisely, as any wandering creature or enemy that gets too close might join in and ruin your best laid plans.


CHARACTERS

Manage an expansive roster of character from eight different classes, each with a unique skill set, both in combat and exploring the world. Choose your party each time you plan to explore your surroundings, depending on the characters available, their status, skills and level of each character and most importantly, your playstyle.

Magic, ranged attacks, sheer power, blocks and stuns, bleed and poisons, magic resistances - all there to explore, use and upgrade.


BASE MANAGEMENT

The Outpost is your base of operations and host to your roster of character. Each building in the outpost gives access to certain features in the game: the Inn to get new quests and find out news, the Apothecary to brew all kinds of potions and elixirs, the “Moon Flower” Burrow to rest and recover your characters and many others. Each building can be upgraded to get access to new features, recipes or improve its functionality.

FEATURES:

  • Explore a rich, diverse and immersive world: beautifully crafted environments - dense forests, haunting dungeons, old forgotten caves, golden deserts and many more.
  • An epic story driving your experience, with rich and expansive lore. Uncover the stories and hidden tales that live on through the people of Zoria and your own companions. Follow the storyline or explore the many stories and side-quests across the world.
  • Plan your moves or just enjoy the ride - a game experience suited both for players eager to just explore and adventure, as well as for those who value planning and strategic thinking. The difficulty levels accommodate both players that just want to enjoy the story and players that welcome a challenge.
  • Easy to learn crafting system with dozens of epic weapons, unique potions and other strange and surprising recipes, all ready to be found throughout the world.
  • Elaborate character management: class and character upgrade system, unique skills destined to both combat and exploration. Many possible character builds for each class, allowing the player to make the party they desire.
  • Complex base management: build new facilities, upgrade buildings, manage resources and expand your base.
